# Blog Post Content Type

A blog post should develop one useful idea with enough context, evidence, and progression that the reader can understand and apply it.

## Content Goal

Create a structured, readable piece that delivers a clear reader shift.

The draft should define:

- the reader's starting problem or belief
- the promise of the post
- the central claim
- the evidence sequence
- the practical or conceptual takeaway
- the ending action

```markdown
## Counterargument or Nuance
[Where the idea can be misunderstood or over-applied.]

```

## Drafting Requirements

The Phase 3 draft should include:

- section purpose
- argument beats
- required examples/data
- transitions
- points to avoid
- approximate word count by section
- phrasing anchors only where they clarify meaning

## Guardrails

- Do not apply voice.
- Do not write polished final prose.
- Do not pad sections without substance.
- Do not use generic SEO filler unless the user explicitly asks for SEO goals.
